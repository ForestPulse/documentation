## Background
Work Package 4.4 focuses on advanced ALS point cloud analysis beyond area-based metrics, targeting individual tree-level information and forest vitality 
indicators. 

Single tree detection and point cloud segmentation methods are applied for crown delineation, with parameterization tailored to tree species 
composition and forest structural characteristics. Additionally, Leaf Area Index (LAI) is derived from ALS data as a key structural-functional trait, 
linking three-dimensional canopy architecture to Sentinel-2-based stress indicators. This integration bridges LiDAR-derived canopy structure with 
high-temporal-resolution satellite observations, improving the accuracy and reliability of forest vitality and health assessments.
